About

Summon Mars is built with restraint and released without warning. Every track is pressure, memory, and motion held just long enough to matter. Nothing here is accidental. Nothing is rushed. If it found you, it was already moving your way.

The work lives in heavy rhythms, disciplined melodies, and lyrics shaped by experience and patience rather than noise. Every track is intentional, carrying weight meant to linger instead of flash and disappear.

The project is developed over years in the background where sound, visuals, and symbols evolved together. Influences run wide, but the execution stays focused.

If the songs feel like they arrived already in motion, that’s because they were never built to chase attention, only to hold it.
